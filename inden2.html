About SportsInfo

SportsInfo is an initiative by InfoCom to deliver clear, fast, and elegant sports coverage. We focus on delivering concise briefs, data-driven insights, and long-form editorial content — all accessible on any device.

This multi-page demo includes Home, About, Sports, Articles, Teams and Contact pages. Every page shares the same modern, minimalist aesthetic and uses Bootstrap for layout.

Mission

To build informative, fast-loading sports pages that are easy to maintain and scale.

Approach

  • Modular components (scorecards, data cards, story layout).
  • Progressive enhancement with optional API hooks for live data.
  • Accessibility and responsive typography.
